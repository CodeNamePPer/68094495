About

SnapCode is a modern coding solutions company founded with the vision to make software development fast, creative, and accessible for the new generation. We specialize in custom software, web, and mobile application development, delivering solutions that are not only functional but also stylish and user-friendly. With a young and dynamic team, SnapCode thrives on innovation and collaboration, ensuring that every project reflects both technical excellence and creative design

News & Events

Smart Community Connect: Connecting Communities with the Power of Technology

On Saturday, September 6, 2025, Sripatum University organized the “Smart Community Connect: Connecting Communities with the Power of Technology” Youth Volunteer Project. Students from five faculties participated in the event, including the Faculty of Accounting, Faculty of Information Technology, College of Logistics and Supply Chain, Faculty of Engineering, and Faculty of Business Creation.

Wai Khru and Student Oath Ceremony

The Faculty of Information Technology, Sripatum University, organized the Wai Khru and Student Oath Ceremony for the Academic Year 2025 on September 18, 2025. First-year students participated to express their gratitude and respect to their teachers, while also preserving and passing on this valuable Thai cultural tradition.
